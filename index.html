About Us

At Angie's Beauty, we are dedicated to the well-being of both the mind body. We offer treatments that target both, believing that the health of one directly relates to the health of the other.

Having opened in 2020, we are excited to combine tried and tested treatments with the more modern side of beauty therapy. We will work with each client on an individual basis and create either a short-term or long-term plan to deliver the perfect outcome for you.

Our skill and passion will help you become your best self.

COVID-19 Safety Information

Due to COVID-19, we have taken precautions to ensure the safety of both our customers and staff.

What you can expect from us
  • Masks will be worn at all times by staff while in the salon
  • All treatment equipment cleaned & sanitised after every service
  • High touch surfaces will be constantly disinfected
  • All treatment chairs/tables sanitised between each service
What we expect from you
  • Masks to be worn in the salon when not undergoing a treatment
  • Hand sanitiser will be provided, please use generously
  • Contact tracing form to be filled out upon arrival to salon
  • Card payment preferred
If you have any questions or concerns about our handling of COVID-19 safety measures, please feel free to contact us here.
