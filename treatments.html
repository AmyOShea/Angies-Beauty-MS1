Brow & Lash Lamination
Brow lamination is a latest trend that gives eyebrows new shape by setting the hair in place.

It is a good alternative to microblading but it is also great in combination with it.

Lash lamination is great for people with straight, or down turned lashes. It is an alternative to lash extention and false lashes.
